Instructions

  • 1. Take a clear picture of your health report ensuring all details, such as text and graphs, are visible and legible.

  • 2. Open your web browser and navigate to our website by entering the URL in the address bar.

  • 3. Once on our website, click on the "Check Report" tab located in the navigation menu to proceed.

  • 4. After accessing the "Check Report" tab, locate and click on the "Upload Report" button to initiate the process.

  • 5. Once you've uploaded your health report, press the "Analyze Report" button and patiently wait for the analysis to complete.
